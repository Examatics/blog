## Q2: What is a PWA and why is it better for students?

**A:** A Progressive Web App (PWA) is a web application that works like a native app but doesn't require downloading from an app store. For students, this means:

- **No storage burden** — doesn't eat up phone storage like 200MB+ native apps.
- **Works offline** — study content cached for use without internet.
- **Auto-updates** — always the latest version without manual updates.
- **Cross-device** — works on any phone, tablet, or laptop with a browser.

Examatics.ai's PWA architecture is specifically designed for Indian students on budget smartphones.

## Q5: How to study on mobile without distractions?

**A:** Mobile study requires intentional design — both from the app and from you:

- **Use a dedicated study app** like Examatics.ai that has no social feeds, ads, or unrelated content.
- **Enable focus mode** on your phone during practice sessions.
- **Keep sessions short** — 5–10 minute microlearning sessions end before distraction kicks in.
- **Practice at consistent times** — anchoring study to daily routines (morning tea, commute, before bed) builds automatic focus.

The microlearning format is inherently anti-distraction: sessions are too short for your attention to wander.
