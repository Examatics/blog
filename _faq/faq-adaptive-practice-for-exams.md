Adaptive practice uses AI to dynamically adjust question difficulty, topic focus, and question types based on your individual performance patterns. ## Q1: What is adaptive practice for exam preparation?

**A:** Adaptive practice is an AI-driven approach to exam preparation where the platform dynamically adjusts what questions you see based on how you perform. Unlike static question banks that serve the same questions to everyone, adaptive practice learns your strengths and weaknesses in real time — then focuses your practice time on the topics and difficulty levels where you need the most work.

---

## Q2: How does AI adjust difficulty in practice tests?

**A:** AI tracks multiple performance signals — accuracy per topic, time-to-answer, error patterns, and consistency across sessions. Based on this data, the algorithm dynamically calibrates the next set of questions:

- **Struggling on a topic?** → Difficulty decreases, foundational questions appear, micro-explanations are triggered.
- **Consistently accurate?** → Difficulty increases, advanced problem types surface, new sub-topics are introduced.
- **Inconsistent performance?** → Mixed difficulty with spaced repetition to solidify shaky concepts.

This creates a practice loop uniquely tailored to each learner.

## Q6: How does AI-powered exam practice work?

**A:** Here's the core loop:

1. **You practice** → answer questions across your exam syllabus.
2. **AI observes** → tracks accuracy, speed, error types, and topic patterns.
3. **Model updates** → your learner profile is recalculated after every session.
4. **Next session adapts** → new questions are served based on your updated profile.
5. **Cycle repeats** → each session is smarter than the last.

This continuous feedback loop means the platform gets better at helping you the more you use it.
